Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

  • How do i log in

    To use the login page, just input your username and password in the respective fields, then click "Login." If you don't have an account, click "SIGN UP" to create one on the registration page.

  • WHere is friend meny?

    Locate the Meny button in the top left corner. When cursor is hovered over, a dropdown meny will be displayed. Press Friends and you will be redirected to the friends meny

  • How to i add friends?

    While in the friend meny, search for the username of the person you would like to befriend in the search field. If the user exist their username should be displayed with a button that says add friend, Press add friend.

  • What do I do if I encounter an error while using the chat system?

    Exit the Website, then reenter the site. Then log in as normal and attempt again. If it still doesent work, contact us.

  • How to contact

    Locate the Meny button in the top left corner. When cursor is hovered over, a dropdown meny will be displayed. Press the Contact button and contact information will be displayed.

Account Questions

  • How do I create an account?

    To create an account, you need to go to register.html and fill in your username, email and password in the respective fields, then click "Sign up".
